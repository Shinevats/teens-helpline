Your Toolkit

Bite-sized tips and tricks to handle whatever life throws at you today. You don't have to figure it all out at once.

📚

Exam Anxiety

Grades don't define your worth. Here's how to chill before a test:

  • Try the 5-4-3-2-1 grounding rule to stop a panic spiral.
  • Break studying into 20-minute chunks (Pomodoro style).
  • Drink water and step away from the screen for 5 minutes.
📱

Social Media Overload

Comparison is the thief of joy. Protect your peace:

  • Mute accounts that make you feel bad about yourself.
  • Try a 12-hour "phone detox" on a weekend.
  • Remember: everyone only posts their highlight reel.
🗣️

Navigating Drama

It's okay to step back from toxic situations:

  • "No" is a complete sentence. You don't always need an excuse.
  • Set boundaries: "I don't have the energy to talk about this right now."
  • Find your true circle—people who let you be you.
💤

Sleep & Rest

Your brain is basically a smartphone; it needs to recharge:

  • Stop scrolling 30 minutes before bed.
  • Try deep breathing: Inhale for 4s, hold for 7s, exhale for 8s.
  • Don't feel guilty for taking a "do nothing" day.
